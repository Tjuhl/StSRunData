DATA MODEL
* FactRun - run related data, e.g. floor reached, character played, ascension level, ...
* FactFloor - floor related data, e.g. floor type, encounter, item purged, gold per floor, health per floor, max hp per floor, ...
* FactFloorItem - floor reward related data, e.g. cards obtained, potions obtained, relics obtained
